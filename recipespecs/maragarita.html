Margarita

Description

The Margarita is a classic cocktail that combines the tart punch of lime with the sweetness of tequila. Always a favorite on a summer day or paired with Mexican food, you can never go wrong.

Ingredients

  • 2 limes
  • 0.5 oz simple syrup
  • 2 oz silver tequila
  • 0.5 oz Cointreau
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine the juice of two limes, simple syrup, tequila, and cointreau in a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Shake until the shaker is ice cold and the ingredients are mixed.
  3. Rub the rim of a glass with lime and rub the rim on a plate containing salt.
  4. Pour margarita into salted glass and enjoy!
