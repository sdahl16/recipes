Gin and Tonic

Description

The gin and tonic is a classic, easy to make cocktail that mixes floral flavors from gin with tangy lime.

Ingredients

  • Half a lime
  • 2 oz gin
  • 6 oz tonic water

Steps

  1. Juice the half of lime.
  2. Combine the gin, lime juice, and tonic in a glass over ice and stir.
